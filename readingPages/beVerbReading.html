Reading with the "Be Verb"

Instructions

On the grammar explanation pages, you can read through explanations of various grammar points. Here, on this page, you will see those explanations in action through reading prompts. These prompts offer a way for you to learn vocabulary while exercising the grammar knowledge that you previously learned. For those of you who may have a higher-level of vocabulary, or those who are interested in learning more difficult vocabulary, you can adjust the vocabulary level by selecting a level from the dropdown menu contained inside the reading prompt box.

This page has a lot to offer, such as the reading prompt, vocabulary, and even audio recordings. The page is layed out in the recommended progression order; However, just doing things once and as they appear won't be too effective. Here is my recommended progression, which should give you a lot more benefit than just blindly progression through each section.

  1. Complete vocabulary flashcards
  2. Read the reading on the default setting (level 01)
  3. Listen to the audio recording of the reading
  4. Look up any additional words you don't know
  5. Reread and relisten to the reading at least one more time
  6. Progress to reading comprehension questions when you are ready
  7. Adjust reading level and repeat process with increased difficulty
